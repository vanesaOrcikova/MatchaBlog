What makes our matcha special?

We source only the finest shade-grown tea leaves from organic farms in Uji, Japan. The leaves are meticulously ground into a fine powder, preserving all the rich antioxidants and L-Theanine for a smooth, focused energy boost without the jitters.

  • Supports detoxification with high chlorophyll content

How to Prepare Traditional Matcha

  1. Sift 1-2 grams of matcha powder into a pre-warmed bowl
  2. Add 70ml of hot water (70-80°C)
  3. Whisk vigorously in W or M motion for 30 seconds
  4. Continue until smooth foam appears on surface
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy
